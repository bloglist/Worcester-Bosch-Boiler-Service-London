Warranty

Importance of regular boiler servicing

Regular servicing of your Worcester Bosch boiler is crucial for several reasons:

Safety

Boilers can develop issues over time, leading to gas leaks or carbon monoxide poisoning. Regular servicing helps identify and rectify potential safety hazards before they become dangerous.

Energy efficiency

A well-maintained boiler operates at peak efficiency, consuming less energy and keeping your bills low.

Boiler lifespan

Regular servicing extends the lifespan of your boiler, ensuring it remains in top condition for as long as possible.

A typical Worcester Bosch boiler service in London should include:
